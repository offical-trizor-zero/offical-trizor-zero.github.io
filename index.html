Trezór Suite | Trezor® Suite App (Official)

The Trezór Suite App is a desktop and web application made for Trezór hardware wallet users. It acts as the control center for your crypto, allowing you to safely send, receive, and track your cryptocurrencies all in one place. With strong privacy features and regular updates, it’s built to keep your digital assets protected at all times.

Q1: Do I need a Trezór device to use the Suite App?

Yes. The Trezór Suite App is designed to work with Trezór Model One and Model T hardware wallets for maximum security.

Q2: Is the Trezór Suite App free?

Yes, it is completely free to download and use.

Q3: Can I use Trezór Suite without an internet connection?

Some features may require internet access, but your private keys always stay offline on your Trezór device.

Q4: How often is the Suite App updated?

SatoshiLabs regularly releases updates with security improvements and new features.
